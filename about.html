Caleb McCarthy is an award winning student Filmmaker from Northern California, attending Cal Poly Humboldt. Caleb writes, directs, and edits his own short films spreading across multiple genres. His works are dark and surreal focusing on stranger characters in strange situations. He has screened internationally at festivals such as Indie Shorts Festival, Mesa International Film Festival, and Alternative Film Festival. He also has worked as a director of photography, editor, sound recordist, and actor.
